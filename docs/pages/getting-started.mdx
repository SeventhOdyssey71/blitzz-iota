## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

### Supported Wallets

IOTA DeFi supports the following wallets:

- **Sui Wallet** (Recommended)
- **Martian Wallet**
- **Ethos Wallet**
- **Suiet Wallet**

### Connection Process

1. Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right corner
2. Select your preferred wallet from the list
3. Approve the connection request in your wallet
4. Once connected, you'll see your wallet address and IOTA balance

## Step 3: Making Your First Swap

### Simple Swap Process

1. Navigate to the **Trade** section
2. Select the token you want to swap from (e.g., IOTA)
3. Select the token you want to receive (e.g., stIOTA)
4. Enter the amount you want to swap
5. Review the transaction details:
   - Exchange rate
   - Minimum received
   - Price impact
   - Network fee
6. Click **"Swap"** and confirm in your wallet

## Step 4: Providing Liquidity

### Why Provide Liquidity?

Liquidity providers earn:
- Trading fees (0.3% of each swap)
- Additional rewards (if available)
- LP tokens representing your pool share

### Adding Liquidity

1. Go to the **Pools** section
2. Select a pool or create a new one
3. Enter amounts for both tokens
4. Review pool share and rates
5. Click **"Add Liquidity"** and confirm

## Step 5: Advanced Features

### Limit Orders

Set specific prices for your trades:

1. Navigate to **Trade ‚Üí Limit Orders**
2. Set your desired price
3. Enter the amount
4. Set expiration (optional)
5. Place your order

### DCA Strategies

Automate your investments:

1. Go to **DCA** section
2. Select tokens and amounts
3. Set frequency (daily, weekly, etc.)
4. Configure price limits (optional)
5. Activate your strategy

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Wallet Connection Failed**
- Ensure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked
- Check if you're on the correct network
- Try refreshing the page

**Insufficient Balance**
- Verify you have enough tokens for the swap
- Remember to account for gas fees
- Check if tokens need to be unwrapped

**Transaction Failed**
- Check slippage settings (try increasing to 1-2%)
- Ensure sufficient gas fees
- Wait and retry if network is congested
