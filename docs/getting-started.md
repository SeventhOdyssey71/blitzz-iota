# Getting Started

Welcome to the IOTA DeFi Platform! This guide will help you get started with trading, providing liquidity, and using advanced features.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

1. **IOTA Wallet** - A compatible wallet like:
   - IOTA Wallet (recommended)
   - MetaMask with IOTA network
   - WalletConnect compatible wallets

2. **IOTA Tokens** - You'll need IOTA for gas fees and trading
3. **Web3 Browser** - Chrome, Firefox, or Brave recommended

## Connect Your Wallet

### Step 1: Access the Platform

Navigate to [iota-defi.com](https://iota-defi.com) (or your local instance).

### Step 2: Connect Wallet

1. Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right
2. Select your wallet from the list
3. Approve the connection in your wallet

### Step 3: Select Network

Ensure you're connected to the correct network:
- **Mainnet** for real trading
- **Testnet** for testing with test tokens

## Your First Swap

### 1. Navigate to Swap

The swap interface is the default landing page.

### 2. Select Tokens

- **Input Token**: The token you want to trade from
- **Output Token**: The token you want to receive

### 3. Enter Amount

Enter the amount you want to swap. The platform will automatically calculate:
- Output amount
- Price impact
- Minimum received (after slippage)

### 4. Review & Confirm

1. Review the transaction details
2. Click **"Swap"**
3. Confirm in your wallet
4. Wait for confirmation

## Understanding Fees

### Trading Fees
- **Swap Fee**: 0.3% per trade
- **Gas Fees**: Variable based on network congestion

### Pool Fees
- **LP Rewards**: Earn 0.25% of all trades
- **Protocol Fee**: 0.05% to the protocol

## Safety Tips

1. **Always verify** token addresses
2. **Start small** when testing new features
3. **Check slippage** settings for large trades
4. **Never share** your private keys

## Troubleshooting

### Wallet Won't Connect
- Ensure your wallet is unlocked
- Check you're on the correct network
- Try refreshing the page

### Transaction Failed
- Check you have enough IOTA for gas
- Verify token balances
- Adjust slippage tolerance

### Prices Not Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Prices update every 30 seconds
